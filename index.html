Política de Privacidade

🔒

Dream Emulator does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is fully protected.

📋

Overview

Dream Emulator is a Game Boy emulator for iOS developed by Thalisson Gabriel. This policy explains what the app accesses and how it is used. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

🚫

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or photos
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Advertising identifiers
📱

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it:

  • Recent games list — stored locally so you can quickly resume your games. UserDefaults
  • Save states — game progress files stored in the app's Documents folder. Documents
  • ROM files — game files you provide, copied to the app's Documents folder. Documents

You can delete all data at any time by removing the app from your device.

📦

ROM Files

Dream Emulator does not include, distribute, or provide any game ROM files. Users are solely responsible for obtaining ROM files in compliance with applicable copyright laws. The app only loads files provided by the user through the iOS Files app or iTunes File Sharing.

🔗

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services. No data is shared with any third party.

👶

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

📝

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
