Privacy Policy

Dream Emulator does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Overview

Dream Emulator ("the App") is a Game Boy emulator for iOS developed by Thalisson Gabriel. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses and how it is used. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Advertising identifiers

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device and never transmits it externally:

  • Recent games list — saved in the app's local storage (UserDefaults) so you can quickly resume your games.
  • Save states — game progress files saved in the app's Documents folder on your device.
  • ROM files — game files you provide are copied to the app's Documents folder for local access.

All of this data remains entirely on your device. You can delete it at any time by removing the App.

4. ROM Files

Dream Emulator does not include, distribute, or provide any game ROM files. Users are solely responsible for obtaining and using ROM files in compliance with applicable copyright laws. The App only loads files provided by the user through the iOS Files app or iTunes File Sharing.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage services. No data is shared with any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no personal data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
